Eligibility Criteria

Basic Requirements

  • Age between 18-65 years
  • Weight above 50kg
  • No chronic medical conditions
  • No recent surgeries
  • No current medications
  • Good general health

Important Notes

  • • Must wait 56 days between whole blood donations
  • • Must not have had a tattoo in the last 4 months
  • • Must not have traveled to certain countries recently
  • • Must not have certain medical conditions

Donation Process

Registration

Fill out necessary forms and provide identification

Health Screening

Quick medical check-up and blood pressure measurement

Donation

The actual donation process takes about 10-15 minutes

Recovery

Short rest period with refreshments provided
